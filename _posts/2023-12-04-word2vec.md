```python
WINDOW_SIZE = 2

data = []
for sentence in sentences:
    for idx, word in enumerate(sentence):
        for neighbor in sentence[max(idx - WINDOW_SIZE, 0) : min(idx + WINDOW_SIZE, len(sentence)) + 1] : 
            if neighbor != word:
                data.append([word, neighbor])
```
